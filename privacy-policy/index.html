Privacy Policy for No Hangs

Effective Date: January 21, 2026

Introduction

No Hangs ("we", "our", or "the app") is a hangboard training application for climbers. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our app.

Data Collection and Storage

No Hangs does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All data created and used by the app is stored locally on your device only.

Information Stored Locally

The app stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Training session data (exercises, reps, weights, dates)
  • Exercise configurations and settings
  • Personal best records
  • App preferences and settings

This data remains on your device and is never transmitted to external servers or third parties.

Bluetooth Connectivity

The app uses Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) to connect to compatible force measurement devices (such as Tindeq Progressor). This connection is used solely for receiving measurement data from the device. No data is transmitted from the app to the device or to any other party.

Third-Party Services

No Hangs does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Bluetooth: Required to connect to compatible force measurement devices
  • Location (Android): Required by Android OS for Bluetooth scanning (no location data is collected or used)

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, your data security depends on your device's security measures. We recommend keeping your device secure with appropriate passwords or biometric locks.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated "Effective Date" at the top of this policy.
